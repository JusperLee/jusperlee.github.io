We present a reinforcement learning framework for optimal lap positioning in warmth-seeking Pomeranians. Our approach models human body temperature distribution, blanket coverage, and proximity to treats as state variables. The resulting policy achieves 100% cuddle satisfaction rate while minimizing the distance to the nearest human at all times.
