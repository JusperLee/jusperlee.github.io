We introduce WarmSeek, a lightweight model for real-time human proximity optimization in companion dogs. By incorporating paw-shake frequency and lie-down timing as attention signals, WarmSeek achieves a 99% human-following accuracy across multi-room home environments, significantly outperforming classical leash-based baselines.
